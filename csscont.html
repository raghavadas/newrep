I once had an Australian guy work for me as a carpenter. Unbelievably he was a qualified Doctor, Barrister, had his commercial pilots license and had modelled on the catwalk during London fashion week. But woodwork was his passion. We checked his credentials and he had it all. After six months he went off touring South America. I often think I will hear from him again as Australian Prime minister.
